Ingredients

1 whole chicken cut up
10 cloves of garlic smashed unpeeled
2 bay leaves
20 peppercorns
1/4 c apple cider vinegar
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 cups water

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Let the chicken soak for 2-4hrs or overnight. Pour everything in a dutch oven and let it boil.

Simmer on medium heat for 1 hour or until chicken is tender. Season with salt to adjust. Serve with warm rice.
